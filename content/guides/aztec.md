## 🗺️ Step-by-Step Level walkthrough

### Stage 1: The Lever & The Slab Bridge
*   **Walkthrough**: In the starting room, you will find two wooden poles and a heavy stone slab standing vertically blocking a gap. Place one pole flat on the ground. Wedge the second pole underneath the vertical slab using the flat pole as a fulcrum. Grab the pole and pull down to tip the massive stone slab forward, making it fall and form a bridge over the gap.

### Stage 2: The Counter-weight Cage Elevators
*   **Walkthrough**:
    1. Walk into the yard with two metal cages suspended from ropes.
    2. Jump inside the first cage. Bob's body weight will cause it to sink, raising the second cage.
    3. Jump from the first cage onto the wall platform, and then jump onto the raised second cage.
    4. From the top of the second cage, jump to the highest ledge. If you want the "Bird’s Eye View" achievement, jump into the nearby tree branches containing a bird's nest and climb to the top.

### Stage 3: The Pendulum Obstacle Course ⭐ Key Puzzle
*   **Standard Walkthrough**:
    1. You will enter a long corridor guarded by heavy stone pendulums swinging back and forth over a narrow, wobbly bridge.
    2. Observe the swinging pattern.
    3. Run as the pendulum swings away from you. Crouch (<span class="kbd-key">Shift</span>) to lower Bob's profile, making it harder for the swings to knock you into the abyss.
    4. Jump across the gaps between swings. To unlock the "Clockwork" achievement, complete this entire course in under 60 seconds.

### Stage 4: Balancing on the Rolling Ball
*   **Walkthrough**:
    1. Locate the massive stone sphere sitting on a ramp.
    2. Grab the sphere and push it to start rolling.
    3. Jump onto the top of the sphere. As it rolls down the ramp, you must walk or run in the opposite direction of its rotation to stay on top, balancing Bob's wobbly limbs.
    4. Keep your balance until the ball rolls into the gap, forming a stable stepping stone to reach the final temple entrance.

## 💡 Pro Tips & Glitch Hacks

<div class="unlock-tip-box">
  <strong>【The Statue Hole Exit Skip】</strong><br>
  You can bypass almost the entire level. In the second area, instead of navigating the cages, use swing-climbing to climb up the decorative stone carvings on the temple wall. From there, you can climb onto the roof of the temple ruins. Walk toward the rear and locate a hidden gap in the geometry. Drop straight through the hole to land directly in the final exit portal!

## 🏆 Hidden Achievements & Easter Eggs

1.  **🗿 The End**: Unlocked automatically by completing the "Aztec" level.
2.  **🦅 Bird’s Eye View**: Climb to the very top of the tree containing a bird's nest located in the counter-weight cage courtyard.
3.  **⚽ Rollin’**: Walk on top of the rolling ball in Stage 4 to cross the gap. You must stay on the sphere without falling off or touching the side walls.
4.  **⏱️ Clockwork**: Complete the pendulum obstacle course section in Stage 3 in under 60 seconds from entering the corridor.
