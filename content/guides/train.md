# 🚂 Level 3: Train

> **Objective**: Maneuver through a series of railway-themed obstacles, manipulate heavy train carriages on tracks, utilize garbage bins to scale heights, and jump into the final red exit carriage.
> **Level Matrix**: Difficulty ⭐⭐☆☆☆ | Est. Duration 12 Mins
> **Mechanics**: Horizontal pull mechanics, carriage friction, high-ledge mantle grabs, and climbing momentum.

## 🗺️ Step-by-Step Level walkthrough

### Stage 1: The First Carriage Blockade
*   **Walkthrough**: After dropping into the level, you will face a yellow train carriage blocking a doorway. Walk up to the carriage, press and hold the <span class="kbd-key mouse-key">Left/Right Mouse Button</span> to grab the side of the carriage, and walk backwards to pull it out of the way. Once the doorway is clear, walk through.

### Stage 2: The Dumpster & Dumpster Skip
*   **Standard Walkthrough**: A large yellow dumpster blocks your path to the next room. Grab it by the handles and pull it backward to clear the doorway. 
*   **Dumpster-assisted Wall Climb**: Alternatively, instead of moving it completely out of the way, you can push the dumpster directly against the high wall on the right. Climb onto the dumpster, run, jump, and grab the ledge of the high wall to pull yourself over, bypassing the next door entirely.

### Stage 3: The Overturned Bench & Flipped Cart
*   **Walkthrough**: In this area, you will find an overturned wooden bench on a raised platform and a train carriage. 
    1. First, flip the bench upright if you want to complete the achievement (see below).
    2. Move to the train carriage. Grab it and pull it away from the wall to create space, or push it against the wall to climb.
    3. Jump from the platform onto the top of the carriage, and use it to reach the higher ledge leading to the next section.

### Stage 4: Carriage on Carriage Alignment ⭐ Key Puzzle
*   **Standard Walkthrough**:
    1. Enter the yard and locate the red train carriage. Climb the staircase to the high platform.
    2. You will notice a yellow train carriage on the tracks below, which is blocking the upper path.
    3. Jump down to the yellow carriage. Grab the side rails, walk backward, and slide it along the tracks until it is positioned directly under the high platform.
    4. Go back up the stairs, walk onto the yellow carriage, and jump across to the platform on the opposite side to reach the exit.

## 💡 Pro Tips & Glitch Hacks

<div class="unlock-tip-box">
  <strong>【Carriage Roof Speedrun Skip】</strong><br>
  Advanced players do not need to slide carriages on tracks. You can perform a wobbly side-climb by grabbing the side panel of the first train cart, swinging your legs up to gain momentum, and pulling Bob onto the roof. From the roof, you can run and jump across the tops of the carriages to skip the entire track puzzles in under 15 seconds!

## 🏆 Hidden Achievements & Easter Eggs

1.  **🧹 Public Service**: Right at the start of the level, you will find a yellow dumpster and 5 pieces of scattered stone debris on the ground. Pick up all 5 pieces of debris and place them inside the dumpster to unlock this achievement.
2.  **🚗 Convertible Ride**: Get inside the dumpster, grab the inner walls, and jump while moving forward. Bob will "drive" the dumpster across the ground. Travel 50 meters in this fashion to unlock the achievement.
3.  **🪑 Perfectionist**: In the third room, find the overturned bench on the sidewalk. Grab it and flip it back upright so that it sits flat and aligned with the wall.
4.  **📡 Maze Whispers**: In multiplayer, if you navigate the dark corners of the train tracks with shadows set to low, you might hear a wobbly developer voice cue echoing through the dream.
