## 🗺️ Step-by-Step Level walkthrough

### Stage 1: Planks & Shattering Glass
*   **Walkthrough**: Start by pulling two wooden planks off the door to proceed. In the next room, the door is locked. Pick up the red fire extinguisher from the floor. Lift it high, and throw it hard against the glass window pane to shatter it. Climb through the broken window frame.

### Stage 2: The Giant Wrecking Ball ⭐ Key Puzzle
*   **Standard Walkthrough**:
    1. You will enter a large yard with a yellow crane control cabin and a swinging bucket wrecking ball.
    2. Climb onto the platform near the wrecking ball. Grab the bucket with both hands.
    3. Swing the bucket backward and forward, using Bob's body weight to build momentum.
    4. Release the bucket at the peak of its swing to crash it into the brick wall on the far side. Repeat until a large hole is smashed. Climb through.

### Stage 3: The Yellow Scaffolding & Gaps
*   **Walkthrough**:
    1. Walk onto the yellow steel scaffolding. Be careful, as these platforms can tilt under Bob's wobbly weight.
    2. Jump across the gap to the concrete floor. Use the lever on the wall to lower the elevator cage.
    3. Step inside the cage, pull the lever again to ascend, and climb out onto the higher balcony.

### Stage 4: Mattress Stacking & The Roof Crane Exit
*   **Walkthrough**:
    1. In the room with the mattresses, you must climb through a high window. You can grab and stack two mattresses on top of each other to create a wobbly ramp, allowing you to walk right up.
    2. Climb onto the roof of the building. Walk along the yellow crane boom.
    3. Grab the hanging hook of the crane, swing off the edge of the roof, and release to drop onto the exit balcony below. Fall into the exit pipe.

## 💡 Pro Tips & Glitch Hacks

<div class="unlock-tip-box">
  <strong>【The Left Window Wrecking Ball Skip】</strong><br>
  You do not need to swing the wrecking ball. Instead of breaking the brick wall, head to the far left ledge of the crane platform. Look toward the window on the left building. Run, jump, and grab the ledge of the window frame. Swing climb your legs up to climb straight into the building, bypassing the wall-smashing puzzle and saving 2 minutes.

## 🏆 Hidden Achievements & Easter Eggs

1.  **🔨 Brute Force**: Unlocked automatically by completing the "Demolition" level.
2.  **🪟 Wrong Direction**: Complete the level by bypassing the wrecking ball puzzle entirely. Climb through the left building's window instead of smashing the wall.
3.  **❄️ Surprise! (Avalanche!)**: In the outdoor construction area, locate a wooden support beam holding back a pile of large boulders. Grab and pull the wooden beam to collapse the structure and trigger the rockslide.
4.  **👊 Primal**: Break 4 breakable walls throughout this level using only manual power (throwing random props, running headfirst into the wall cracks, or body-slamming) without using the wrecking ball or fire extinguishers.
