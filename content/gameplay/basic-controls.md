# 🎮 Basic Controls Guide

In Human: Fall Flat, Bob's wobbly body has two independently controlled arms. Every keyboard/mouse input is converted into torque output for his wobbly muscles. This guide will help you build muscle memory and master the fundamental movement physics.

## ⌨️ Input Command Terminal

Familiarize yourself with the default key bindings to coordinate Bob's limbs:

*   **Move Walk**: Use <span class="kbd-key">W</span><span class="kbd-key">A</span><span class="kbd-key">S</span><span class="kbd-key">D</span>.
*   **Rotate Head/View**: Move your <span class="kbd-key">Mouse</span>. Bob's hands reach toward the center of your crosshair.
*   **Left Grab**: Hold <span class="kbd-key mouse-key">Left Mouse Button</span>. Left hand sticks to the target.
*   **Right Grab**: Hold <span class="kbd-key mouse-key">Right Mouse Button</span>. Right hand sticks to the target.
*   **Jump**: Press <span class="kbd-key">Space</span>. Jumping carries forward momentum when moving.
*   **Run**: Hold <span class="kbd-key">Shift</span>. Bob bends over slightly and accelerates.

---

## 📐 Mechanics & Core Movement Systems

### 1. Grab & Release System
*   **The Physics**: As long as you hold down the <span class="kbd-key mouse-key">Left/Right Click</span>, Bob's hands act as high-strength sticky pads. Any surface (walls, boxes, teammates) will be stuck tight.
*   **Tip**: Release the mouse buttons to let go. When carrying objects, keep the hands flat and pull backwards to slide items smoothly.

### 2. Standard Wall Climb (Step-by-Step)
This is the most critical hurdle for new players. Master this 4-step sequence:
1.  **Approach & Look Up**: Move up to the wall, look straight up at the sky, and raise both hands high.
2.  **Jump & Grab**: Press <span class="kbd-key">Space</span> to jump. Ensure both hands stick to the top ledge of the wall. Keep holding the mouse buttons!
3.  **Pull Up (Look Down)**: Slowly move your mouse down to look at the ground. Bob will naturally pull his torso up to the ledge.
4.  **Forward Over**: While looking down, press and hold <span class="kbd-key">W</span>. Bob will swing his legs over and climb on top.

---

<div class="unlock-tip-box">
  <strong>【Climbing Tip for Beginners】</strong><br>
  Many beginners fail because they release the mouse buttons too early when trying to step onto the ledge. Remember: <strong>Never release your mouse clicks until both feet are firmly on top of the wall!</strong> Keep looking down until Bob climbs over.
