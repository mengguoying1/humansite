⛰️ Level 5: Mountain

Objective: Scale vertical stone cliffs, push a red train carriage to create a bridge, search a pitch-black cavern for hidden gems, swing across a massive ravine using a rope, and throw loudspeakers into a house. Level Matrix: Difficulty ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ | Est. Duration 20 Mins Mechanics: Ledge mantle climbing, object manipulation, swinging rope physics, and cave navigation.

📸 Mountain Ledges: Scale wobbly stone pillars and learn the art of mantle climbing.

🗺️ Step-by-Step Level walkthrough

Stage 1: The Winding Stone Ledges

  • Walkthrough: You start at the base of a series of stone pillars. Follow the S-shaped path. Reach high with both hands, jump and grab the ledge of the next rock step. Look down at Bob’s feet while holding the clicks, and hold W to pull yourself over. Continue until you reach the grassy upper ridge.

Stage 2: The Red Train Carriage

  • Walkthrough:
    1. On the ridge, you will find a large red boxcar sitting on rails.
    2. Grab the boxcar and pull it backward to clear the gap on the left, or climb directly onto its roof by using its corner bars.
    3. Run along the top of the boxcar, jump across to the adjacent cliff, and follow the path to the entrance of a dark cavern.

Stage 3: The Dark Cave & Gem Hunt

  • Walkthrough:
    1. The cave is pitch-black. Grab the lantern sitting near the entrance.
    2. Carry the lantern to light your path. You will need to jump over a series of gaps inside the cave.
    3. If you are going for the “My Treasure” achievement, search the dark corners of the cave to find 7 glowing green gems and pile them together.
    4. Exit the cave onto a high ledge overlooking a deep ravine.

Stage 4: The Tarzan Rope Swing ⭐ Key Puzzle

  • Standard Walkthrough:
    1. Look up to locate a yellow rope hanging from a tree branch above the canyon.
    2. Walk to the edge of the cliff. Jump, stretch your arms, and grab the rope.
    3. Swing back and forth to build momentum.
    4. At the peak of your forward swing, let go of the grab keys and jump forward to land safely on the rocky platform on the opposite side.
    5. Follow the pathway up to a cottage, pull open the door, and fall into the exit.

💡 Pro Tips & Glitch Hacks

【One-Handed Swing Climbing (Wall Climbing)】
You can scale almost any vertical cliff face in Mountain using the swing-climbing exploit. Grab the rock wall with one hand. Move your camera left and right to make Bob's body swing. When Bob swings upward, quickly grab higher up the wall with your free hand. Let go with the lower hand and repeat. This allows you to climb straight to the summit, skipping Stage 2 and Stage 3 entirely!

🏆 Hidden Achievements & Easter Eggs

  1. ⛰️ What Goes Up: Unlocked automatically by completing the “Mountain” level.
  2. 💎 My Treasure: Gather all 7 glowing green gems hidden inside the cavern and place them in a neat pile. You will need the lantern to locate them in the dark crevices.
  3. 🐒 Tarzan Swing (AH, EO, EO, EO, EO, OOOOO!): Use the rope in Stage 4 to swing across the ravine. Ensure you grab the rope cleanly and swing all the way to the other side to trigger the achievement.
  4. 📢 Silent Hours (Noisy Neighbours): Outside the exit cottage, you will find a set of loudspeakers. Grab the speakers and throw them through the nearest window of the cottage.
