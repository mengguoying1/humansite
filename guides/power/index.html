⚡ Level 9: Power

Objective: Connect colored electrical cables to circuit ports, operate heavy industrial machinery including forklifts and dump trucks, haul coal to feed furnaces, and ride high-velocity cooling fans to the exit. Level Matrix: Difficulty ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ | Est. Duration 30 Mins Mechanics: Electrical cable routing, forklift operation, heavy vehicle transport, wind draft lift, and boiler pressure systems.

📸 Grid Control: Connect batteries to power doors and drive industrial vehicles.

🗺️ Step-by-Step Level walkthrough

Stage 1: Wiring & The Short-Circuit

  • Walkthrough: Walk up to the starting wall panel. Grab the red cable plug and connect it to the red socket; grab the blue cable plug and connect it to the blue socket. This powers the door. If you want the “Electricity 101” achievement, swap the plug connections to link a single wire directly into both positive and negative terminals (connecting blue to red socket) to trigger an explosive short-circuit.

Stage 2: Forklift Controls & The Statue Battery

  • Walkthrough:
    1. Enter the next yard to find a yellow forklift. Stand on the seat, grab the steering wheel to drive it, and slide the metal forks under the heavy metal roller gate.
    2. Operate the lever to lift the forks, opening the gate.
    3. In the courtyard, locate the giant stone statue holding a glowing battery in its hand.
    4. Climb up the statue’s pedestal, scale its arms, and knock the battery out of its grip to claim it.

Stage 3: Coal Hauling & Boiler Feed ⭐ Key Puzzle

  • Standard Walkthrough:
    1. Locate the dump truck. Drive it back to the dark coal quarry/cave.
    2. Use your hands to pick up pieces of coal and toss at least 10 pieces into the bed of the truck.
    3. Drive the truck back to the generator building. Dump the coal near the furnaces.
    4. Feed the coal into the open furnace chambers to generate steam pressure.

Stage 4: Floating on Aerodynamic Fans

  • Walkthrough:
    1. Connect three batteries (including the one stolen from the statue) to the main power terminal to power the giant ceiling fan.
    2. Stand directly under the fan. As it starts rotating, the upward wind draft will lift Bob into the air.
    3. Float across the deep void, using your movement keys to steer Bob toward the exit hatch on the far platform.

💡 Pro Tips & Glitch Hacks

【Forklift Gate Climbing & Fan Blade Launch】
You do not need to operate the forklift. By parking it against the gate and climbing onto its roll cage, you can reach the top of the wall and jump over, skipping the lever puzzles. Additionally, in the fan room, you can perform a wobbly wall-run along the pipes on the side wall to jump directly to the exit platform, skipping the battery connection.

🏆 Hidden Achievements & Easter Eggs

  1. ⚡ The End?: Unlocked automatically by completing the “Power” level.
  2. 💥 Electricity 101: Short-circuit the starting panel by connecting a single cable between the positive and negative terminals.
  3. 🗿 Thief: Steal the battery held by the giant stone statue in the courtyard.
  4. 🔋 Will It Fry: Feed a single appliance (such as the main generator door) with power from 3 batteries simultaneously.
  5. 🏎️ Petrolhead: Cumulative distance achievement. Drive the forklift or the dump truck for a total distance of 1 kilometer around the map.
  6. 🚚 Delivery Boy: Load 10 pieces of coal into the dump truck and successfully deliver them to the main island boiler room.
