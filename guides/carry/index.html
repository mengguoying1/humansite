📦 Level 4: Carry

Objective: Transport wooden boxes across the level, place them on glowing pressure plates to trigger electronic doors, use boxes as step-stools to scale high glass walls, and fall into the exit. Level Matrix: Difficulty ⭐⭐☆☆☆ | Est. Duration 10 Mins Mechanics: Lifting and carrying physics, manual positioning, box-assisted climbing, and door jamming.

📸 Carrying Box: Lift wooden boxes using both hands to trigger pressure buttons on the floor.

🗺️ Step-by-Step Level walkthrough

Stage 1: The Entry Button

  • Walkthrough: Drop into the level and locate the small wooden box on the floor. Raise both arms, grab the box, and walk over to the large yellow floor button. Place the box directly on top of the button to open the glass sliding door.

Stage 2: Through the Grated Window

  • Walkthrough:
    1. In the next room, you will see a box on one side of a locked glass door, and the floor button on your side.
    2. Grab the box, place it on the floor button to open the door, and walk through.
    3. Now, you need to bring the box with you. Grab the box through the open gap or window in the wall, lift it up, and pull it to your side of the room.

Stage 3: The Elevator Lift & Door Jam

  • Walkthrough:
    1. You will reach a section with a raised platform and a sliding gate that only opens when you stand on a button.
    2. Place the box on the button. Since the box keeps the door open, walk through.
    3. If you want the “It’s Stuck” achievement (see below), place the box right under the sliding door so it jams when the button is released.
    4. Carry the box onto the cargo elevator platform, press the button to go up, and transport it to the final area.

Stage 4: Scaling the High Glass Wall ⭐ Key Puzzle

  • Standard Walkthrough:
    1. In the final area, you are faced with a very high glass railing wall that you cannot climb normally.
    2. Bring the wooden box close to the glass wall.
    3. Climb onto the top of the box. From this elevated position, look up, jump, and grab the ledge of the glass wall.
    4. Pull Bob over the ledge and drop into the exit vortex below.

💡 Pro Tips & Glitch Hacks

【Railing Skip & Double-Hand Mantle】
You do not actually need the boxes to climb the glass wall. By running along the side railing of the elevator platform, jumping at the absolute edge, and throwing both hands up, you can grab the lip of the high glass wall. Swing Bob's body back and forth, and climb straight over. This skips the entire box carrying process in Stage 4.

🏆 Hidden Achievements & Easter Eggs

  1. 🪵 Don’t Get a Splinter!: Unlocked automatically by completing the “Carry” level.
  2. 🚪 It’s Stuck: In Stage 3, take a wooden box and place it precisely in the middle of a sliding door’s path. Walk off the button to let the door close onto the box. Walk through the jammed doorway to unlock the achievement.
  3. 🗼 Tower: Stack all 4 wooden boxes in this level on top of each other. This is easier in multiplayer: one player holds the base box stable while the other lifts and places the remaining boxes. Make sure to keep your camera pointed downward when dropping the boxes to align them perfectly.
